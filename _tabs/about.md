## Why Kryptosis?

*kryptos* (Greek) — hidden, concealed 

*osis* (Greek) — a state, process, or condition

Kryptosis: The ongoing process of living privately and securely.


The pursuit of digital privacy is not a sprint. There is no single finish line.

Digital privacy is a mindset — a framework you build and refine, one day at a time. The path is long and uneven, and progress is measured in small, deliberate steps: a habit changed, a risk understood, a tool adopted. No single step transforms everything, but each one moves you forward.

This blog exists to walk that path with you — offering clear strategies and honest advice as you cultivate a life of digital privacy.

One step at a time.
